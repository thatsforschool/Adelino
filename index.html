Adelino has several years of experience with wine tasting and port wine tasting, small and large.
We hold wine tastings for staff associations, wine clubs, birthdays, neighbors, groups of friends, and much more.
In addition, we hold planned wine tastings classes in our premises in Danmarksgade, where you can sign up.
These tastings vary between different content and themes.
Wine tasting can also take place "out of the house".
